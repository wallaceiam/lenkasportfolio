About Me

I am a UX Designer who has worked for large organisations such as Royal Bank of Scotland, Lloyds or Amazon. I transferred into the UX profession from running large business change projects.

I am driven to create, improve and deliver user experience by implementing UX processes from research to release for digital experiences (mobile/desktop) and thrive in a customer-centric environment.

With my infectious enthusiasm, I take a great deal of pride and joy in guiding others to work together to improve themselves and their community.

Being a very hands-on person, I have developed a number of UX and technical skills including user testing and research, persona creation, journey mapping, IA, visual design, wireframing in Sketch, Marvel or Adobe Xd, prototyping in InVision and Axure and coding in HTML and CSS.
