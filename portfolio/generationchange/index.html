Generation Change

A Responsive Website that encourages users to create an impact and social youth action in a community.

Project Overview

The Brief

  • Create a responsive design for desktop and mobile devices
  • Re-design the identity of the existing website
  • Refine visual elements and evolve the tone of voice
  • Promote Generation Change organisation itself and their work
  • Engage their target audience and increase their social media presence
  • Act as the go-to website for access to free resources that enable their target audience to adopt, recognise and spread social change
  • Promote youth social action and help to grow this movement

The Problem

  • Overall website navigation needs re-design
  • Find out more about the work of Generation Change is unclear
  • "About Us" section is just one long page with lots of text
  • Access to Resources requires more logical solution and more efficient navigation for target audience
  • User Journeys needs to be quick and easy without confusion
  • "Impact Accelerator" course section lacks interaction and coherence
  • Events section needs to be added
  • Lack of engagement through social media and newsletter

The Solution

Generation Change has now its new face through a responsive design for desktop, tablet and mobile. With a fresh pair of eyes, the existing interface was re-designed in order to promote Generation Change as a striking youth social action organisation that differs from the rest.

The overall website navigation has improved by a large amount and “About Us” section was re-designed. “Resources” and “Course” pages were completely redefined. Events, newsletter pages were added and social media presence was enhanced.

The final prototype was received very well by the client.

I hope that the new site will bring more traffic to Generation Change website, create more impact and social action.
