Bloomsbury Beginnings

Mobile Web App helping people with mission driven ideas who want advice, accountability and support from sustainable enterprise start up experts and a perfect place to collaborate with others on a similar journey

Project Overview

The Brief

  • To re-brand the visual identity of the brand
  • To re-design the key pages of the current Bloomsbury Beginnings website
  • To help promoting sign up to the course, its value and longer term relationship with support group
  • To create new features for existing members of Bloomsbury Beginnings including supportive peer-networking, event opportunities, engagement, accessible resources
  • To help users growing their sustainable business ideas by creating an app they can benefit in great amount

The Problem

Main website

  • To increase revenue by attracting new customers
  • To highlight the business value proposition and make it more understandable
  • To promote the educational programmes and their value
  • To create a fresh and easy course booking process
  • To bring the course members together by promoting one of the key objectives of safe and supportive place
  • To highlight professional skills in-house including external experts and their focus on collaboration with each other

New Members area

  • To increase engagement, peer-networking place, and confidence boosting
  • To create a vibrant community and interactive tools where users can continue their journey and develop their business propositions

The Solution

This mobile web app creates an exciting opportunity for more individuals who want to grow their sustainable business ideas further and make this world a better place. The key focus was to attract more potential customers to sign up for the course and to develop members area. All members would benefit from a range of features including access to experts, peer-networking, confidence boosting, motivation and vibrant community. Moreover, they can benefit from goal tracking, business plan updating on the go and continuous development.
